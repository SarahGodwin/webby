VIRTUOSI PRODUCTIONS JAZZBEAT TRIO

Virtuosi Productions works with Ottawa’s top jazz musicians.

Jazz musicians love to mix and match in all kinds of combos, but Virtuosi Productions’ most popular jazz combo is THE JAZZBEAT TRIO—guitar or piano, flute and/or sax, and bass. Playing elegant mainstream jazz, smooth pop and Latin jazz, THE JAZZBEAT TRIO is the perfect background for corporate and government events, cocktail receptions, product launches, wedding ceremonies and receptions, holiday parties (especially Christmas!) and anniversaries—virtually any special occasion or social event.

Also available are combos including clarinet, trumpet or flugelhorn, trombone, jazz violin and/or voice.

Virtuosi Productions books jazz musicians to play for Canada’s Prime Ministers and Governor Generals, as well as for numerous private and corporate events.
